Training Laboratory

Are you considering mvc vs. webforms? Looking to get new hires up to speed on the Agile Development Process? Does your team need one-on-one HTML5 training? Our public courses are 3 day (4 day for NServiceBus) boot camps running from 8:30am to 5pm here in Austin. Can't make the trip? Ask about our virtual attendance option.

Find an upcoming session or workshop on Eventbrite: http://headspring.eventbrite.com/

We also offer custom and on-site courses, developed specifically to fit the needs of your team. These courses can cover anything in our standard trainings, as well as front-end technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and JQuery. Just let us know what you need.

 
RavenDB Training Curriculum
RavenDB is a transactional, open-source Document Database written in .NET that allows high-performance, low-latency applications to be built quickly and efficiently. In this hands-on course, developers learn how to use the quickly evolving Document Database so they can save time and effort when communicating with database storage.
MVC 4 Training Curriculum
Created by Jimmy Bogard and Jeffrey Palermo, this course covers common development pitfalls and the ins and outs of writing maintainable systems. Instead of listening to lectures and doing boring written exercises, participants jump in and work on real code in an existing application. Our curriculum covers the latest version of MVC 4 and mobile web development.
NServiceBus Training Curriculum
Used in production since 2006, NServiceBus is the most popular open-source service bus for .NET and is found across industries including finance, healthcare, retail, SaaS, web2.0, and more. In this class, developers learn to use all the latest features, including the right ways to implement message exchange patterns and sagas. We'll also talk about architecting for integration, retries, fault tolerance, and scaling out processes across server farms.
