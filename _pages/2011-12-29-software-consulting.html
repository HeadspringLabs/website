Technical Design

We look to give each of our partners the right solution to their toughest businesses challenges. To do so, with no surprises, requires dedication and commitment to upfront planning. Let us take care of the planning for you. The Technical Design service we provide will typically involve (based on our customers’ needs) the components opposite.
Business Analysis
Ensures your custom software will be the right solution for the problem that needs solving. Understanding your business and its processes is the catalysis for innovative design that will exceed end user expectations.
Goal Setting
Collaboration with project stakeholders to define and priortize business objectives for the engagement and determine applicable ways to measure success.
Risk Identification & Mitigation
Answering the question “what could go wrong?” and developing plans to mitigate the identified risks. This is just another way we strive to achieve software development with no surprises.
Feature Backlog Creation & Prioritization
This is the creation of a detailed list of features categorized by functional area, that are then ordered to maximize the value of the project for the company.
Project Milestones and Incremental Releases
Headspring believes the most important tenant of agile software development is to “release early and often.” We will work with the stakeholders to identify incremental production releases points. This allows for earlier return on investment and the incorporation of valuable feedback at the most vital stage from end users.
Architecture Definition
Defining an elegant system architecture, that will maximize the opportunities for automated regression testing, is our passion and a key to producing maintainable applications. With our years of experience in this activity the process is quick and extremely effective.
Key Tool Choices
Determining the best development tools and third-party libraries for a project is a crucial element to creating software effectively and allows for our software to remain within budget and time constraints. We use many tools to aid in development and you can see them in more detail on our tools and processes page.
Staffing Assignments
Another important piece of the software development puzzle lies in selecting the correct team size and members for specific roles and responsibilities. If you choose to develop with Headspring, your team will be carefully selected based on the Headspring consultants that are best suited for your particular project.
Cost Estimation
Once the above tasks have been completed, we are able to give our customers a precise estimate on what the development of the project will cost. With Headspring we are so confident our technical design phase is accurate we provide you with a “not-to exceed” bid. Meaning the number we give is the maximum amount you will ever pay for the defined project. If it costs less then you pay less.

Coaching

You know you have the talent in house to complete the project, but perhaps they are stuck in the weeds and need an outside perspective to get back on track. If your IT organization is having trouble progressing with its legacy application, Headspring is here to help you find success. Partners of Headspring have mentioned these trouble areas when utilizing Headspring’s coaching program.
Improving Developer Productivity and Quality
We will give your developers the tools to:
  • Perform Repeatable, Streamlined Deployments
  • Enhance Configuration Management
  • Improve Development Environments
  • Deepen Team-Focused Engineering Practices
  • Implement Agile Project Management Methodologies
Troubleshooting System Performance Issues
We will teach your team to perform system checks and how to diagnose and fix the following problems:
  • Database Performance Issues
  • Application Stability
  • System Performance Problems
Increasing Long Term Scalability and Maintainability
To ensure your system has the ability to keep up with future growth and changing business needs, we mentor teams to:
  • Write code that uses SOLID principles
  • Work with tools that allow for easy maintenance
  • Create processes that are repeatable and automated

Technical Assessment

Are you hitting a brick wall with your application development? Do you have a system in place but things aren’t going as smoothly as you would like? Or are you looking to invest in a technology company and want to see the quality or potential of the business?

Our experience has enabled us to see many different systems and understand what make a system successful. If you are in a position that leaves you saying yes to the above questions, a Headspring Technical Assessment can help you get answers.

Team Assessment
Assesses the relevant capabilities of each member on a development team. Relevant capabilities include: project strategy, project management, business analysis, system and application architecture definition, software development, and software configuration management.
Process & Tool Assessment
Compares the development practices of a team against established best practices and reviews tool choices for maximum efficiency. Best practices include: automated unit testing, automated acceptance testing, source control management, continuous integration, automated deployment, and automated database change management.
Technology Assessment
Appraises the strengths and weaknesses of a software design and then recommends how to increase software quality, decrease maintenance and improvement costs, and improve system performance attributes such as average response time and throughput.
