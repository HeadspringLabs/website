This series is a guide to pragmatic MVC – based web
 development. After a thorough overview, the text dives into issues of architecture and maintainability. The book assumes basic knowledge of ASP.NET (v.3.5). Some of the topics covered:
  • How to effectively perform unit and full-system tests.
  • How to implement dependency injection using StuctureMap or Windsor.
  • How to work with the domain and presentation models.
  • How to work with persistence layers like NHibernate.
  • More than you ever wanted to know about ASP.NET MVC 4
