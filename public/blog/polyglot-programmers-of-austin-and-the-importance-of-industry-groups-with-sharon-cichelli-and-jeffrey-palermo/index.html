    Industry Groups With Sharon Cichelli, the Founder of Polyglot Programmers of Austin

    In this cast, Sharon Cichelli and Jeffrey Palermo discuss the importance of industry groups, both the benefits of participating in, and volunteering for them.

    Sharon is a Senior Consultant for Headspring.  Aside from building valuable custom business software, Sharon writes a blog at GirlWritesCode.com, builds unusual home furnishings with Arduinos, and plays too much pinball. She founded Polyglot Programmers of Austin, a co-working study group which encourages developers to branch out and learn other languages and platforms.
