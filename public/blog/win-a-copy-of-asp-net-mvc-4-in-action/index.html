Win a Copy of ASP.NET MVC 4 in Action

Hey everyone, the wait is over! Jeffrey Palermo and Jimmy Bogard’s newest book, ASP.NET MVC 4 in Action, is finally available.  To celebrate the release, Headspring is giving away 3 free copies!  We’re taking entries for the drawing all week, and winners will be announced next Monday (June 11th, 2012). Also, if you’re attending Code Camp Austin this weekend, be sure to stop by the booth for an additional chance to win.  

If waiting is not an option, and you must read it before your friends, don’t sweat it. You can purchase your own copy through the publisher: http://www.manning.com/palermo3/

Good luck!

UPDATE (6/11/2012):

The winners are in! Congratulations to  Robertson Sagolsem, David Perkalis, and Jamie Cross. Check your email for information on receiving your copy.

To those who missed out, check your email tomorrow for a special consolation prize :)

Thanks for playing!
