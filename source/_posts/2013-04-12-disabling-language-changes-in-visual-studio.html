It was typical "big enterprise" setup with support for multiple languages. I discovered there's a keyboard shortcut "Ctrl+Space" for changing your language that I would keep accidentally activating in Visual Studio. This post explains how to disable that.
  • Go to the control panel.
  • Choose "Region and Language".
  • Click the "Keyboard and Layout" tab.
  • Click on "Change Keyboards".
  • Click on "Advanced Key Settings".
  • Look over the list of sequences, ensure they are all set to "none" for keyboard activation.
