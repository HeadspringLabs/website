Headspring is honored to invite you to a free half-day conference hosted by the Austin chapter of Entreprenuer Organization (EO) at Headspring’s headquarters.

The EO24 event will feature world-renowned speaker Sharon Birkman who will talk about The Birkman Method®, an industry-leading personality assessment tool that allows people to achieve accurate self-awareness through reliable metrics.

Participants will receive a full personality assessment profile valued at nearly $500 that teaches self-motivation, how to be more effective managing stress behaviors and minimizing stress reactions, areas to excel, contribute, communicate and lead based on assessment results.

Seating is limited to 75 people and only 28 spots remain.  A personality assessment test must be completed by Monday, November 5, 2012 and can be accessed after completing the online registration process.  If you’d like to attend, please register at http://eoaustin24.eventbrite.com.

About Entrepreneurs’ Organization

The Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) is a dynamic global network of more than 8,000 business owners in 40 countries. Headspring CEO Dustin Wells is president-elect of EO Austin. Founded in 1987 by a group of young entrepreneurs, the year 2012 marks EO’s 25th anniversary. With its transformative experiences and unparalleled peer network, EO transforms the lives of the entrepreneurs who transform the world. EO is the catalyst that enables entrepreneurs to learn and grow from each other, leading to greater business success and an enriched personal life.
